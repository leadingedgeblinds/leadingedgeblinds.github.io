  faq_items:
  - title: Do you offer free in-home consultations?
    text: Yes. We provide free, no obligation in home consultations across Melbourne. During the visit, we assess your space, take measurements, and help you choose the best product for your needs.
  - title: How long does production take after I place my order?
    text: Production times vary by product, but custom shutters typically take 6–10 weeks, while blinds and awnings generally take 2–4 weeks. We will advise you of expected timelines during your consultation.
  - title: Do you handle installation?
    text: Absolutely. Our professional installers manage every part of the installation process, ensuring your products are fitted accurately and operate smoothly.
  - title: What areas of Melbourne do you service?
    text: We service most Melbourne suburbs and surrounding areas. Contact us with your postcode to confirm availability.
  - title: What are the payment options?
    text: We accept major payment methods including credit/debit cards and bank transfer. A deposit is typically required to begin production of custom orders.
  - title: Do your products come with a warranty?
    text: Yes. All our products include manufacturer and installation warranties. Warranty length varies by product type, and we will outline all details during your consultation.
  - title: Can I get help choosing the right product for my home?
    text: Yes. Our consultants provide personalised advice based on lighting, insulation needs, style preferences, and your home’s interior design.
  - title: How do I clean and maintain my shutters or blinds?
    text: We provide maintenance guides for each product type. Most shutters and blinds require simple dusting and occasional wiping with a damp cloth.
  - title: What happens if something goes wrong after installation?
    text: We offer ongoing after-care support. If you experience any issues, simply contact us and we’ll organise a service visit or provide guidance to resolve the problem.
